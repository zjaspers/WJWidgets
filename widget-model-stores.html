What Model Stores Do Differently
🌅 Morning Task Review (Before Store Opens)
League City TX: Manager reviews task list at 8:45am every day. Assigns owners, sets 2-hour completion windows, flags blockers. Takes 10 minutes. Results in 88%+ on-time completion.
🔄 Weekly Back-of-House Owner Rotation
Concord NC: Rotates inventory control ownership weekly. Builds skills across the team, prevents burnout, maintains 93% completion rate on inventory tasks.
📣 Daily Huddle Task Accountability
Charlotte NC: 5-minute team huddle at 10am. Reviews yesterday's completion %, calls out wins, assigns today's priorities. Team knows what's expected before floor opens.
💬 Manager Engagement Sets the Tone
All three stores: Manager reacts to every team post within 2 hours, comments weekly, shares team wins publicly. High engagement = high execution (proven 3.2pt lift).
